About Me

Hi, I'm Andy. I am a Computer Science student at the University of North Carolina Greensboro. This portfolio showcases my skills and projects. I will update this site as I gain more knowledge and experience.

Skills

Web Development

I have several years of web development experience. During high school, I worked at a company that made websites for small businesses using WordPress. Additionally, I have worked on several personal and school projects using full stack development skills. I have made websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap for front-end and Java, Python, Node.js for the backend. I’ve also used SQL and MongoDB for databases. I’m always looking to broaden my web development skills with each new project.

Programming

I have been programming for 5+ years. I was self-taught for the purpose of game development until I started taking classes in high school and college where I am getting my degree in Computer Science. I have experience programming as both a solo developer and as part of a group. I have worked on websites, Chrome extensions, standalone applications, and games. Over time, I've used langauges such as Java, Python, JavaScript (and TypeScript), C, and SQL with some very light use of C++ and C#.

IT

I’m always keeping up with the newest developments in technology and figuring out how to incorporate those into my work and life.
