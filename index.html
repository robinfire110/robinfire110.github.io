Skills

Web Development

I have several years of web development experience. During high school, I worked part-time at a company that made WordPress websites for small businesses. Additionally, I have worked on several personal and school projects using full stack development skills. I have made websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap for front-end and Java, Python, Node.js for the backend. I also have experience working with SQL and MongoDB databases.

general Programming

I have been programming for 5+ years. I was self-taught for the purpose of game development until I started taking classes in high school and college where I am pursuing my degree in Computer Science. I have experience programming as both a solo developer and as part of a team. I have worked on websites, Chrome extensions, standalone applications, and games. Over that time, I've used languages such as Java, Python, JavaScript, C, C++ and SQL.

Game Development

I started learning how to use GameMaker Studio in 2016 and have been making games sense. I’ve participated in several game jams and have slowly been working on a large scope RPG project. While most of my experience is within the 2D confines of GameMaker, I also have some experience in 3D development. Using BabylonJS, I made a VR game for a university course and I’ve dabbled with Unreal Engine. To learn more details about my game development endeavors, you can go to my website or itch.io page.

Computers & IT

From a young age, I have had an interest in computers. I independently researched and tinkered with technology to find out all there is to know. This has resulted in many general IT skills such as experience building computers, troubleshooting software and hardware issues, proficiency with Linux and Windows, tech support skills and even some InfoSec knowledge from a part-time internship. I’m always keeping up with the newest developments in technology and figuring out how to incorporate those into my work and life.
