About Me

Hi, I'm Andy. I love technology and I have a lot of experience tinkering around with many different things. This portfolio showcases my skills and projects (listed below). I will keep this page updated as I acquire new experiences.
Thanks for stopping by!

Skills

Web Development

I have several years of web development experience. During high school, I worked part-time at a company that made WordPress websites for small businesses. Additionally, I have worked on several personal and school projects using full stack development skills. I have made websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and React for the front-end and Java, Python and Node.js for the backend. I also have experience working with SQL and MongoDB databases.

Game Development

Videogames have been a passion of mine ever since I was young and the thought of being able to make them was a big motivation for pursing a career in technology. I started learning how to make games in 2016 and have participated in several game jams and have slowly been working on a large scope RPG project. While most of my experience is with 2D projects in GameMaker Studio, I've also dabbled in 3D development. Using BabylonJS, I made a VR game for a university course and I’ve worked through an online course using Unreal Engine. To learn more details about my game development endeavors, you can go to my game development website or itch.io page.

Programming

I have been programming for 8+ years. I was self-taught for the purpose of game development until I started taking classes in high school and college, ultimately resulting in a degree Computer Science. I have experience programming as both a solo developer and as part of a team. I have worked on websites, Chrome extensions, standalone applications, and games. Over that time, I've used languages such as Java/TypeScript, Python, Java, C, C++ and SQL.

Computers & IT

From a young age, I have had an interest in computers. I've independently researched and tinkered with technology to find out all there is to know. This has resulted in many general IT skills such as experience building and servicing dozens computers, troubleshooting software and hardware issues, proficiency with Linux, Windows and MacOS, tech support skills and even some InfoSec knowledge from a part-time internship. I’m always keeping up with the newest developments in technology and figuring out how to incorporate those into my work and life.

And more...

Outside of computers, programming, and software development in general, I have many other interests. I love music, in my free time I compose, listen and perform music. I enjoy playing videogames along with keeping up with the gaming industry. I also adore film of all kinds. TV shows, Hollywood movies, animation, anime, anything that can tell an engaging story is right up my alley.
